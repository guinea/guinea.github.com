Momoka is a platforming adventure game set in a constellation of planets, presented in a vibrant low-polygon style. You control the heroine Momoka as she sets off on a quest to discover why her star is dying. Gameplay seamlessly switches from a solid platforming shooter within and between each of the planets, to exploration of the large planetary system using her spaceship.
